# Retail Sales Dashboard – Power BI

This is an interactive Power BI dashboard created to analyze a retail sales dataset.
The project focuses on understanding sales trends, profit performance, customer segments, regions, and product categories.

## Project Overview

The dashboard includes:

. Total Sales, Profit, Quantity, Discounts, Shipping Cost

. Sales & Profit by Year

. Profit by Category (Technology, Office Supplies, Furniture)

. Profit by Region

. Sales by Market (APAC, EU, US, etc.)

. Customer Segment-wise Sales

## Skills Used

. Power BI

. Power Query (data cleaning)

. DAX (basic calculations)

. Data visualization

. Business insights

## Key Insights

. Technology is the most profitable category.

. Consumer segment generates the highest sales.

. Central region leads in profit.

. Sales increased steadily from 2011–2014.
